About Us

Who We Are

Dar at Tawheed ek Islami markaz hai jo taleem, ilm aur hidayat ko aam karne ke liye kitaabein aur brochures chap kar logon tak pohchata hai. Yahan aapko Islami taleemaat par mabni asli aur mufeed kitaabein milengi, jo roohaniyat, ibadat aur insaniyat ke behtareen pehlu samjhati hain.

Our Purpose

Hamara maqsad hai ke har ghar tak deen ki roshni aur sahih ilm pohche. Dar at Tawheed ki zyadatar kitaabein mashhoor Islami aalim Sheikh Irfan Surti sahab ki tasneefaat hain, jo aam logon ke liye asaan zubaan me samajhne layak hoti hain.

Our Mission

Humari mission yeh hai ke asli aur authentic Islami kitaabein logon tak qeemat par available ho jayein. Is ke liye hum All India courier service ke zariye har jagah kitaabein bhejte hain.

Our Vision

Hum yeh chahte hain ke har shakhs apni zindagi ko deen ki roshni se roshan kare, aur har ghar tak Qur’an aur Sunnat ka paighaam pohche.
